Biography

I am currently a researcher at Smartmore. I obtained my Ph.D. degree at Computer Science and Engineering Department, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), supervised by Prof. Jiaya Jia and Prof. Yu Bei. Before that, I received the B.E. (Honors) degree from Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT) in 2018.

My research interest includes computer vision and deep learning, especially the 2D scene understanding and efficient semantic segmentation.
