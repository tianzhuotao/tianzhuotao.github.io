Biography

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher in Max Planck Institute for Informatics (MPI-INF), working with Prof. Bernt Schiele. I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Engineering Department, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), supervised by Prof. Jiaya Jia and Prof. Chi-Wing Fu. Before that, I received the B.E. degree from Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT) in 2017.

My research interest includes computer vision and deep learning, especially the 3D scene understanding and efficient feature learning.
